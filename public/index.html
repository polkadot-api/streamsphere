What we do

In the past, we've launched trading platforms with substantial monthly volumes and have contributed and led the development of a plethora of significantly influential open-source libraries and projects.

Now we're combining our expertise to provide you with modular libraries, optimize your DApp performances, and equip our users with elite tools to better navigate the world of data.

The Team

Josep M Sobrepere

Josep, a key contributor at Parity Technologies since September 2021, played a vital role in projects like @substrate/connect, Polkadot-API, and more. As the architect of Polkadot-API, he transformed @substrate/connect into the first Polkadot Provider compliant extension. Beyond Parity, he co-authored the React-RxJS project with Victor Oliva and contributed to prominent open-source libraries, including RxJS, React-Router, React-Redux, and Reselect.

Victor Oliva

Víctor, a seasoned software developer specializing in real-time data, has been a consultant since 2016, with a focus on building trading platforms. Collaborating with Josep, he co-authored React-RxJS, using it as the primary state management library to successfully launch two trading platforms. One platform achieved a monthly volume of 12 billion USD with around 3500 filled orders per day. Víctor has also made significant contributions to various open-source projects, including RxJS, chai, SockJS, webpack, and fakehost. His diverse side projects range from exploring alternative approaches to handling decimal numbers to developing a rollup plugin for Polkadot-API.

Our Libraries

Polkadot-API:

Polkadot-API aims to surpass PolkadotJs by offering meticulously crafted, composable libraries aligned with a "light-client first" philosophy. It empowers decentralized app developers, enhancing the overall developer experience and setting a new standard for efficiency, adaptability, and performance in the Polkadot ecosystem.
